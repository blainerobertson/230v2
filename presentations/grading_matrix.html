Teaching Presentation Grading Matrix

Teaching Presentation Rubric
Objective 3 - 10 Points Objective 6 - 10 points
5 points — The teaching presentation must provide information about the topic so that the audience can learn about the topic from your materials. 4 points — The written material in the presentation are grammatically correct and error free.
5 points — The teaching presentation must contain a working example with an explanation such that the example can be understood enough to be reproduced and used by the audience. 3 points — Verbal communication in the presentation is clear and focused on the topic being presented.
  3 points — The physical presentation is concise, focused and paced to fit within the allowed time (7 minutes).
