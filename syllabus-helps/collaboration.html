Collaboration

Collaboration - the ability to work together to accomplish a task, goal or outcome - is a skill that is consistently mentioned by employers as a requirement for new and seasoned employees. In this course you will be requried to work with others on a weekly basis using both the discussion board and team meetings. Each individual is responsible for her or his own work, but interacting, supporting, assisting and collaborating are desireable and required in this class.

Learning Team

During week 2 you will organize yourselves into teams of 3 or 4. The team will become your primary source for feedback and help. You are required to meet together each week to discuss the content of the week, assessments, insights and help one another with these things.

Discussion Board

Each week has a discussion board for the entire class. You are encouraged to use the discussion board weekly. Specifically, you ask questions, answer questions or share outside resources. Your participation is truly assisting the class and its members to move forward!

Be a builder and a contributor via the boards!
