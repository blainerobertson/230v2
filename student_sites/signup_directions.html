Adding Yourself to the Student Site List

In order to gain access to your work it requires that your web site is easy to find. The same is true of everyone in the class. Click the Student Site List (it links to an online spreadsheet) page in this module and enter your name (Lastname, Firstname) in column A beneath the "Student Name" heading.

Once your name is in the cell (Lastname, Firstname), turn it into a hyperlink pointing to your website. You will need to provide a full path (e.g. http://mydomain.github.io). The directions to do this are shown below:

Create a Hyperlink

  1. Click on the cell to add the hyperlink
  2. From the Insert menu, select "Link"
  3. In the dialog box that opens enter the full address in the "Link" text box
  4. Click the "Apply" button
  5. When done, test the link by hovering over the link text and clicking the pop-up that appears near it
  6. Your page should open in a new tab

This is the place

When your site needs to be visited by another member of the class or by the professor or teaching assistant, this will be where we will go to do so. Make sure the link works and is accurate.

Editing the Link

If you ever need to update the link, simply click in the cell and repeat the step of creating the link, but making the adjustments to the URL as needed.
