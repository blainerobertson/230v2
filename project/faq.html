Project FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions regarding the final project:

How can I check the weight of a web page?
Navigate to the page you want to check, then click the YSlow tool. Click the "Run Test" button. Check the components tab for the weight of the page.
A second method is to open the "Inspect" tool in Chrome or the "Inspect Element" tool in FireFox. Click the "Network" tab. Reload the page. Examine the "# transferred" in the tool. These #'s will be measured in kilobytes. You want your number to be less than 400.
What are the usability concepts mentioned in the grading matrix?
Usability concepts are those things that make our page content more friendly and useful to a site visitor. Specific items are listed in the Usability Checklist.
Can I use YouTube or Vimeo to house my overview video?
Yes. In fact that is the suggested way of providing the video. Then, you will submit the URL to the video in the dropbox comment at the time you submit your project code. You can make your overview video "Public" or "Unlisted", but it must NOT be designated as "Private".
